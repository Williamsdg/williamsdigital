Our Story

A Birmingham Tradition
Since 1996

Continental Bakery began with a simple belief: that a great loaf of bread can bring people together. Founded in the heart of Birmingham's English Village, we've been baking European-style breads and pastries using time-honored techniques for nearly three decades.

Our bakers arrive before dawn each day, mixing doughs by hand, shaping each loaf with care, and baking in our stone-deck ovens imported from France. We use only the finest ingredients — unbleached flours, European-style butter, and natural starters that have been nurtured for years.

What started as a small neighborhood bakery has grown into one of Birmingham's most cherished gathering places, but our commitment to quality and community has never wavered.

— The Continental Bakery Family
