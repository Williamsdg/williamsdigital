Our Story

Where Coffee
Meets Culture

Pink Lantern is Birmingham's go-to destination for Vietnamese-inspired coffee and craft cocktails. By day, we serve rich, bold Vietnamese coffee alongside matcha lattes and specialty teas. By night, we transform into a vibrant cocktail bar with creative drinks inspired by flavors from around the world.

Located in the heart of Birmingham at 1st Ave North, we're more than a coffee shop — we're a community space where everyone is welcome.
