Welcome to Chez Lulu

A Funky & Eclectic
French Café

Nestled in the heart of Birmingham's English Village, Chez Lulu brings you the flavors and spirit of French cafe society. Our menu encompasses the whole Mediterranean region — from northern Spanish tapas and northern Italian handmade pastas to northern African cuisine.

Every dish features fresh produce and top quality ingredients, with our breads baked daily in our hearth right next door at Continental Bakery.

Right Next Door

Continental Bakery

All desserts are made from scratch right next door at Continental Bakery, also owned and operated by proprietor Carole Griffin. From delicate pastries to celebration cakes, every creation reflects our commitment to quality and tradition.

  • All desserts made from scratch
  • Fresh pastries baked daily
  • Artisan breads for the café
  • Celebration cakes & special orders
