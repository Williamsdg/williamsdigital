Our Story

A Railroad Park
Tradition

Nestled at the Regions Bank 17th Street Plaza, The Boxcar Cafe is Birmingham's favorite outdoor dining destination. Our name pays homage to the park's railroad heritage, with our venue designed to echo the scale and charm of a classic railway boxcar.

Now operated as Michael's @ The Boxcar, we bring restaurant-quality cuisine to the park's relaxing atmosphere. From our signature Cuban sandwich to ice-cold craft beverages, every visit is a taste of what makes Birmingham special.
